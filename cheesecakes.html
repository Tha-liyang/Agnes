"Fiadone (Corsican Cheesecake) with Chestnut Honey and Figs"

Brocciu cheese, a fresh cheese made from sheep or goat milk, is frequently used in the making of fiadone, a Corsican cheesecake. It comes from the Mediterranean island of Corsica and is a classic dessert. Typically, citrus zest and occasionally herbs like mint are used to flavor cheesecake. Chestnut honey and figs would improve the dish by bringing in sweet and earthy notes that would provide a great counterpoint to the tanginess of the cheese.

"Limoncello-Ricotta Cheesecake"

The sweet and tart flavors of limoncello, an Italian lemon liqueur, are combined with the rich, creamy flavor of ricotta cheese to create a delectable delicacy. This gives the traditional cheesecake a bright, lemony twist. While the ricotta cheese gives the cheesecake a lighter texture than typical cheesecake, the limoncello offers a distinctive flavor character.
